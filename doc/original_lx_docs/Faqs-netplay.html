Net' Play FAQ's

Q1. How do I play LieroXtreme online?
A1. Run LieroX.exe and on the main menu you have to click "Net' Play".

Q2. How do I join a server?
A2. While you are un the Net' Play section, click the "Internet" button at the top of the screen. In that screen click "Update List", click the server you wish to play, then click the "Join" button.

Q3. How do I start a server of my very own?
A3. On the main menu screen after running LieroX.exe, click Net' Play then click the "Host" button At the top of the screen. There you can choose the server name as people would view it on the Server List screen, Choose the Max amount of players that can join at once (Most is 8) and click the "Register-Server" box to make it so the server will show up in the server list screen.
Once you have set those options you can add the player you wish to play by double clicking the character in the left box, then press "Ok" in bottom right hand corner. You may now change the game settings by pressing the "Game Settings...." button and you can also view who is playing on your server and there's a chat box there also.
Now simply press "Start" which will take you to the weapon select screen.
