How To Play

Movement
Your worm can be moved in two directions; left and right. The worm can also aim up and down, and can also jump.
By default, the controls for player 1 will be R, D, F, G. Holding the keys D or G will move the worm left and right respectively.
Your worm can also jump up by pressing the jump button (left alt for player 1). To move around a level full of dirt, the worm has the ability to dig.

Digging is performed by holding the direction button you want to move (eg, right) and tapping the opposite direction button (eg, left).
For example, if I wanted to dig right, i'd hold G to move right, and tap D.
Digging is also effected by the aim of the worm. A worm can dig up or down within limits.


Shooting
By default, the shoot key for player 1 will be the left ctrl. Holding this key will fire the weapon the worm currently has selected.
To aim were the weapon will fire, use the up and down buttons (R, F for player 1) This will move the crosshair up and down within certain limits
To change the current weapon, hold the weapon select button (left shift for player 1) and use the left and right buttons to cycle through the five weapons.
When the weapon runs out of ammo, it must reload. The time of reloading depends on the type of weapon, and the loading times of the game.


Ninja rope
The ninja rope is useful for getting to places around the level quickly, especially in caverns were digging up is too slow.
The ninja rope can be thrown by pressing the ninja rope button (Z for player 1). The hook will be thrown until it hits something in the level (rock, dirt) or there is not enough rope left.
Once hooked onto something, the rope will pull the worm in the direction of the hook.
To release the rope, press the ninja rope button to fire the rope again, or press jump to completely release the rope.


Bonuses
Bonuses randomly drop around the level every 30 seconds. A bonus can be a weapon crate or a health pickup.
When you pick up a weapon crate, the weapon inside will replace your current weapon. If you have currently selected a big nuke, you don't want to be replacing it with a handgun or something small.
Health pickups simply add health to your worm.
