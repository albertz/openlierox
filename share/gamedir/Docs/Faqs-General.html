General FAQ's

Some parts of Liero Xtreme don't work! Whats wrong?
Liero Xtreme is still in alpha stages. More then likely, the part of the game you are referring to has not been coded, or is yet to be finished.

I can't see any human players to play as
You have to add a player before you can play the game. Click on 'Player Profiles' on the main menu and add a player.

I can't add any worms to the playing list
You have to double click on a worm in the players list to add it to the playing list. To remove a worm from the playing list you must double click on it.

Feature [insert feature here] from the original Liero is missing in this version
Some of the features from the Original Liero i didn't implement, and the rest are yet to be coded in
I've only used the original Liero game to get ideas from. I havn't tried any downloadable mods for Liero, so some ideas may be good, but I don't know about them.
